health is
our mission

We reduce food insecurity by growing, cooking, and serving food in sustainable packaging to vulnerable community members in need.
‍
The health of those who are experiencing homelessness in our community is our focus.
‍
Successfully supporting them means we have to provide them with nutrients. It’s not just about eating; they need nourishment.

funding transparency

Our funding is broken down into three categories–food, supplies, and labor.

Fair pay for our staff (not volunteers) enables them to stay committed to the job.
‍
Commitment to the job means meal consistency and program sustainability.

your support
keeps us alive

100% of our funding comes from you.
Ten dollars a month will sponser three fresh meals for a fellow community member.
‍
Our relationships with local chefs and farms, enables us to deliver meals with the highest quality of ingredients which ensures that their surplus product does not go to waste.
